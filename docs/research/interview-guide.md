# Interview Guide (6–8 participants)

**Duration:** 25–30 min  
**Audience:** Knowledge workers who use ChatGPT or similar for work tasks weekly+

---

## Warm-up (5 min)

1. How do you use AI assistants today? (research, writing, coding, other)
2. What was the last time an AI answer was **wrong** or **misleading**? What happened?

---

## Core (15 min)

3. When you get an answer, what do you do to decide if you can **use** it?
4. Do you read citations or sources when the tool provides them? Why / why not?
5. Would a **per-claim** review (Trust / Unsure / Reject) help or feel like extra work?
6. For high-stakes work (client email, report, job application), what would you need before trusting an export?
7. What’s worse: (A) tool says “high confidence” but is wrong, or (B) tool refuses to answer?

---

## Concept test (5 min)

Show NL ChatGPT prototype (or screenshots):

8. What’s confusing? What’s valuable?
9. Would you use this before sending something important? What’s missing?

---

## Close (2 min)

10. Anything else about judging AI quality we didn’t ask?

**Record:** participant role (anonymized), date, top 3 quotes, severity of pain (1–5).
